Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

The Unplugged retreat is located in the serene mountains just outside Oakridge. It's a two-hour drive from the nearest city, and we recommend carpooling with your team. Detailed directions will be provided in your confirmation email, along with an emergency contact number.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

The Unplugged retreat is ideal for teams that want to deepen their connection, improve collaboration, and focus on creativity without digital distractions. Whether you're a tech startup, a creative agency, or a non-profit, any team ready to unplug and reset will thrive here.

What size teams can you accommodate?

We can comfortably accommodate teams ranging from 5 to 25 participants. For larger groups, we recommend reaching out to discuss special arrangements. Our goal is to create an intimate and impactful experience, so we limit the total capacity for any retreat.

Is there really no wifi available?

That’s right—there’s no Wi-Fi available at the retreat! This is a deliberate choice to help participants disconnect from their devices and fully immerse themselves in the experience. However, a landline is available for emergencies, and we encourage everyone to inform their contacts about their offline time.
